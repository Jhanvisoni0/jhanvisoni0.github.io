AI Isn't Sitting on Top of Databricks Anymore

August 2026 · Jhanvi Soni

AI isn't just sitting on top of Databricks anymore. It's being woven into every layer of the platform.

Here's what that looks like in practice:

AI/BI Genie — your AI coworker

Business teams can ask questions in plain English and get insights directly from governed data. Less dependency on SQL experts. Faster access to analytics.

Genie Ontology — the context layer

AI is only as good as the context behind it. Ontology helps agents understand business concepts like "revenue," "customers," or "churn" based on how your organization actually defines them.

Agent Bricks — build domain-specific agents

Create AI agents on top of your governed lakehouse for use cases like fraud detection, pipeline monitoring, and anomaly investigation.

Unity AI Gateway — govern AI at scale

A centralized layer for controlling models and agents with access policies, monitoring, and guardrails.

The common thread? Context closer to data. Governance closer to AI.

The future of data engineering isn't just pipelines, it's building trusted foundations where AI can reason and operate safely.
